> Tic-tac-toe (also known as noughts and crosses or Xs and Os) is a paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.

### Development
- Using  MinGW's GNU GCC 5.1.0 
- There are two options to play aginest:
  - Another player

     > Player One always plays with 'X'
  
     > Player Two always plays with "O"

  - CPU

    > Human always plays with 'X'
  
    > AI always plays with "O"
    - Easy Level
        - AI always plays randomly.
        
    - Medium Level
        - AI always Defending and try to attack if it's available.
